Our Services

Extractions

A safe procedure to remove a tooth that is severely damaged, fractured, or causing spacing issues. It is performed to relieve pain, treat infections, and prepare your mouth for future restorative treatments.

Root Canals

Also known as endodontic treatment, it is the ideal solution to save a tooth whose pulp (nerve) is infected. The inside of the tooth is cleaned and sealed to eliminate pain and preserve your natural tooth.

Cleanings

Essential for complete oral health. In this preventive procedure, we remove accumulated plaque and tartar that daily brushing cannot, helping to prevent cavities, gum disease, and bad breath.

Braces

Classic orthodontic treatment to correct teeth alignment and improve bite. Braces gradually move teeth to their ideal position, achieving a more aesthetic, functional, and healthy smile.

Dental Implants

The most advanced solution to replace a lost tooth. It consists of a titanium post that acts as an artificial root, on which a crown identical to a natural tooth is placed, permanently restoring function and aesthetics.

Fillings (Resins)

Aesthetic restorations used to repair teeth with cavities, minor fractures, or to improve their shape. Being tooth-colored, they blend perfectly with your smile, offering a durable and natural result.
